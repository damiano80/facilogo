FACILOGO
What is this?
Facilogo is the new open source, free for all, open to contributes human language.
What's new about this language?
Facilogo has some ideas taken from the world of computer programming: it aims to have no ambiguity, be schematic, easy to use and understand and fast to be learned.
Why?
I'm not a good foreign language speaker, when it comes to talk and listen. Italian is my mother tongue and it has its pro and cons. I cherry picked some nice ideas from other languages to compose my ideal language and I hope it will become popular and appreciated as I think it can be.
